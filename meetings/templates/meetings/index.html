How CisspMeetings works?

CisspMeetings provides all the functionality you need to meet with other cissp students online and study togather for the exam.

Create an account

Create an account and then log into your account.

In your account Dashboard

Are you an admin or a user?

There are two types of accounts user and admin. You will know which account you have when you log into your account.

03

Do you have admin account?

Do you have admin account?

If you have an admin account. you will have to create a zoom meeting and then publish it on your Dashboard. So that the other students can join the meeting.

03

Do you have user account?

Do you have user account?

If you have a user account. you will find a link to the zoom meeting in you your Dashboard. if you didn’t find the link, just wait until the admin publishes it.
