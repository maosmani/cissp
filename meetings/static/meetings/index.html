Get started

How it works

Weekly Meetings:

Each week, you will meet with two CISSP students on zoom call.

  • You will explain to them what you have learned in the last week, So that they can learn from you about the topics that you have studied in the last week.

  • They will explain to you what they have learned in the last week, So that you can learn from them about them about the topics that they have studied in the last week.

  • At the end of the meeting, you will ask them questions about the things that you don't understand, And they will try to answer you.
